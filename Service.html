Our security measures include:

Data Encryption: Protecting sensitive information with advanced
encryption techniques.

Secure Authentication: Implementing multi-factor authentication
(MFA) and role-based access control (RBAC).

Regular Security Audits: Conducting periodic assessments to identify
and mitigate vulnerabilities

DDoS Protection: Deploying advanced firewall systems and intrusion
